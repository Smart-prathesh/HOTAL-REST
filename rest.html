Indian Accent

Indian Accent is an award-winning fine-dining restaurant known for its innovative and contemporary Indian cuisine. It offers a modern twist to traditional Indian dishes and has earned a reputation for its impeccable service and elegant ambiance.

Bukhara (at ITC Maurya)

Bukhara is a legendary restaurant located at the ITC Maurya hotel. It is renowned for its North Indian cuisine, especially its succulent kebabs and signature dishes like Dal Bukhara. The restaurant's rustic and traditional decor adds to its charm.

Chokhi Dhani, Jaipur

Chokhi Dhani is a famous ethnic village resort that offers an authentic Rajasthani dining experience. Visitors can enjoy traditional Rajasthani dishes served in a village-style setting with cultural performances and entertainment.

Spice Court, Jaipur

Spice Court is a well-known restaurant in Jaipur, serving a variety of Rajasthani, North Indian, and Mughlai dishes. The restaurant's ambiance reflects the region's cultural heritage, making it a favorite among locals and tourists.
